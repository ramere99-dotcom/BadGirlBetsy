Where Vintage Meets Soul

Nestled in the heart of Invermay, Bad Girls Betsy's is more than just an antique store—it's a curated journey through the golden eras of design. We specialize in authentic mid-century modern furniture, retro décor, and quirky collectibles that tell a story.

From Eames-inspired chairs to atomic-age lamps, every piece in our collection is hand-selected for its character, quality, and that undeniable cool factor. Whether you're a serious collector or just looking for that perfect statement piece, we've got something that speaks to you.
